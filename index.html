About Me.

I am currently a final year Digital Media student, at the University of Salford. I have a passion for creating and designing, with my main interests focused in UX/UI Design, Web Design and 3D Modelling. I also have experience in a variety of different media, as seen in my portfolio.

I aspire to create innovative, engaging and accessible content, whilst promoting new ways of retaining information in the most efficient and considerate way.
